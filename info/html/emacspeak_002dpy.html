11.131 emacspeak-py

This speech-enables python-mode available on sourceforge

11.131.1 emacspeak-py Commands

Interactive Command: emacspeak-py-next-block nil

Move forward to the beginning of the next block.

Interactive Command: emacspeak-py-previous-block nil

Move backward to the beginning of the current block. If already at the beginning then move to previous block.
